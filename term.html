Q. What will be the period for delivery of products?

A: We care for the customers’ requirements and therefore, the delivery of products will be made within 15 days from the date of booking of the product. However, as ours is a handicraft product, there may be a delay of a week or two but only in exceptional situations.


Q. Can a customer place request for a complete custom order on a new fabric?

A: Certainly, yes as customer satisfaction is our utmost priority. Moreover, promotion of Madhubani paintings is the primary goal for us. To place a request, just call us and the products will be prepared as per your choice of design, colours & fabric!


Q . What is the replacement policy?

A: We have very simple replacement policy for products. A buyer can replace the product purchased, if the same is not in accordance with the order placed, within 10 days of its delivery and can get it replaced, wherever possible. However, kindly note that the design of the product will be same as per the selected code but there may be a slight variation in the colurs of the paintings. The slight variations are because the paintings are done manually. Replacement is subject to the non-use of the product in any manner.


Q. Why to buy from us?

A: We vouch for originality of our products based on Madhubani paintings. These are hand-made products. The designs are first made by the person, who has been conferred with a National Award for this very craft. Thereafter, these designs are painted on the products by rural women. However, they are skilled in Madhubani paintings. So, in a way, buying these products from us also means contributing to the well-being of the least-privileged people of the society.
